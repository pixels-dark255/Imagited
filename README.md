# Imagited
INTRODUCTION

Imagited explores new ways to turn waste materials into valuable manufacturing resources. The project combines recycling, engineering, and digital fabrication to enable sustainable production.

PROCESS
1. **Collection & Cleaning:** Used PET bottles are collected, labels removed, and bottles cleaned and dried to remove contaminants.

2. **Processing:** Bottles are cut into strips or shredded and then heated and pulled through an extruder to form **1.75 mm plastic filament**.

3. **Filament Use:** The recycled filament is cooled, spooled, and used in **3D printers to make new products**, turning plastic waste into useful materials. ♻️
